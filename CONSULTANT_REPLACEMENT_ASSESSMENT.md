## What AFAQ Cannot Do Yet (Consultant Still Needed)

### 1. Stakeholder Engagement ‚ùå
**Consultants:** Interview employees, suppliers, customers to identify material topics
**AFAQ:** Relies on user-provided inputs only

**Impact:** Materiality assessments (required by IFRS S1) need human facilitation.

### 2. Industry-Specific Expertise ‚ùå
**Consultants:** Know construction sector ESG risks vs. tech sector risks
**AFAQ:** Generic framework mapping, no sector intelligence

**Impact:** Output feels "templated" rather than "expert-crafted" for niche industries.

### 3. Strategic Roadmaps ‚ùå
**Consultants:** "Here's your 3-year decarbonization plan with milestones"
**AFAQ:** "Here's your current emissions and the gap to net zero"

**Impact:** Tool outputs compliance reports, not strategic action plans.

### 4. Audit Defense ‚ùå
**Consultants:** Will defend the report in front of auditors, answer questions, revise
**AFAQ:** Produces a document, but no one to stand behind it in audit

**Impact:** Companies still need internal ESG lead or consultant on retainer for audit season.

## What Needs to Happen to Hit 90% Replacement

### P0 (Must Have)
1. **Framework Registry Expansion:** Populate 30-50 requirements per major framework (GRI, IFRS S1/S2, UAE SCA)
   - **Impact:** Currently only ~6 requirements = looks like demo, not real tool
   - **Effort:** 2-3 weeks with ESG analyst or AI agent

2. **PDF/Word Export:** C-suite signs off on PDFs, not HTML dashboards
   - **Impact:** Without this, AFAQ is a "draft tool" not a "deliverable tool"
   - **Effort:** 1 week (use jsPDF or server-side generator)

3. **Evidence Upload/Attachment:** Let users attach policies, data files, photos as evidence
   - **Impact:** Auditors ask "Where's the proof?" ‚Äî evidence = credibility
   - **Effort:** 1 week (Supabase Storage integration)

### P1 (Should Have)
4. **Materiality Wizard:** Guided interview-style tool to identify material topics
   - **Impact:** Unlocks listed company use case
   - **Effort:** 2 weeks

5. **Gap Analysis ‚Üí Action Plan:** "You're missing X, here's how to fix it by deadline Y"
   - **Impact:** Shifts from "compliance checker" to "strategic assistant"
   - **Effort:** 1 week

6. **Human Review Layer:** Optional paid service ($499) for expert to review output before submission
   - **Impact:** Bridges trust gap for first-time users
   - **Effort:** 2 weeks (marketplace integration + vetting)
