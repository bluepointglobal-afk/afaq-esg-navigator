# Requirements Taxonomy (Internal Guidance Only - V0)

**Goal:** Improve credibility by defining a small requirements taxonomy per jurisdiction (UAE/KSA/Qatar). This document is intended for internal AFAQ ESG Navigator guidance and does not constitute legal advice.

---

## 1. Requirements Taxonomy by Jurisdiction

### UAE (United Arab Emirates)
*   **Corporate Governance Baseline:**
    *   **Regulator:** Securities and Commodities Authority (SCA).
    *   **Reference:** Decision No. 3/RM/2020 (Corporate Governance Code for PJSCs).
    *   **Expectations:** Mandatory Board composition (independent/non-executive), Audit & Risk Committee oversight, Conflict of Interest policies.
*   **ESG Reporting Expectations:**
    *   **Frameworks:** SCA Sustainability Report (Annual), ADX ESG Disclosure Guidance, DFM ESG Reporting Guide.
    *   **Expectations:** Mandatory annual sustainability report for PJSCs within 90 days of year-end. Alignment with GRI/TCFD encouraged.
*   **Risk Controls (Cyber/BCP):**
    *   **Expectations:** Board oversight of Risk Appetite, Information Security policies (SCA), and alignment with UAE Net Zero 2050 (Environmental Risk).

### KSA (Kingdom of Saudi Arabia)
*   **Corporate Governance Baseline:**
    *   **Regulator:** Capital Market Authority (CMA).
    *   **Reference:** Corporate Governance Regulations (updated 2023).
    *   **Expectations:** Shareholder rights protection, Board responsibility definitions, mandatory Social Responsibility policy (Article 87).
*   **ESG Reporting Expectations:**
    *   **Frameworks:** Tadawul ESG Reporting Framework, CMA ESG Disclosure Guidelines.
    *   **Expectations:** Voluntary but highly encouraged; shift toward mandatory reporting via the Unified National ESG Guidelines (in progress).
*   **Risk Controls (Cyber/BCP):**
    *   **Expectations:** Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) frameworks, alignment with Saudi Vision 2030 sustainability objectives, SAMA Cyber Security Framework (for financial entities).

### Qatar
*   **Corporate Governance Baseline:**
    *   **Regulator:** Qatar Financial Markets Authority (QFMA).
    *   **Reference:** Corporate Governance Code for Listed Companies (Decision No. 5 of 2025).
    *   **Expectations:** Boards of 7-11 members, minimum 3 independent directors, mandatory Risk Management and Compliance Committee.
*   **ESG Reporting Expectations:**
    *   **Frameworks:** QSE ESG Guidance, QFMA 2025 Code.
    *   **Expectations:** Mandatory evaluation and disclosure of ESG performance in annual reports (effective August 2025).
*   **Risk Controls (Cyber/BCP):**
    *   **Expectations:** QCB Technology Risk Instructions (2022), activation of BCP (ISO 22301), and cybersecurity alignment (ISO 27001).
